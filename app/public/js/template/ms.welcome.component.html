Welcome to ImprovPlus.

ImprovPlus applies Improv to Talent Development. We have distilled our most popular Improv workshops into comprehensive guides for Facilitators and Trainers. We walk you through the activities, the agenda, the questions, and the discussions that will bring out the best in your team.

ImprovPlus is also a flexible method that can be integrated into your existing training equation. As an ImprovPlus Facilitator, you will have an innovative approach to establish ground rules, set the tone, and lead your trainings with confidence. We introduce you to the ImprovPlus way and we leave you with room and flexibility to conduct your trainings your way.

These tools can help anyone apply Improv to training. No Improv experience is necessary - all you need is the trainer.

(Don't have one? You can bring us in to lead an ImprovPlus training, too.)
